Projects

  • The Legend of Zelda (Unity Remaster)
This is a remaster of the NES Legend of Zelda in Unity. It was developed using C# and Visual Studio for script creation, Photoshop for novel sprite creation, and the BeepBox website for novel music arrangements/sound effects. Press the 1 button for cheats, and the 4 button for a new level and mechanic. In this project, I was responsible for most aspects of development except for enemies - and I also worked on a couple of enemies like the Blade Trap. My partner did have to rework a few things related to enemies as he went through enemy development, but I built the groundwork for all facets of game that you see in the finished project. I also developed the new mechanic, designed the level, and created the appropriate sprites/sounds. Enjoy!
